Just for fun :)

It was during the second half of my first year that our professor wanted us to pick something we felt passionate about and take it further and to keep pushing ourselves throughout the semester. Back then I was interested in North Korea and the old communist posters, why not create a satirical poster? Doesn't seem that intimidating now does he?

Aside from using tonnes of cardboard and multiple laser cutting sessions and trying to find my way in figuring out how to create a blog on Wordpress (oh, first year me), I've arrived to this stencilled product alongside with a website promoting human rights in the DPRK that has now ceased to exist online anymore. Apologies for that.
