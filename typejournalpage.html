A journey from East to West

Our assignment was to design a publication that typographically documents one day of our life. The subject matter was up for us to decide, however the information must be organized in a typographic manner. No images were allowed but only typographical elements such as grid, hierarchy, paragraph, character styles etc.

I chose to document my flight from Hong Kong to New York (22 hours). Information incorporated were in-flight activities, news from the ground, and when that was not available, I substituted that with a short info about the place/country.
