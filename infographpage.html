Graphing population and passports

Through the use of opacity and positioning, the graph conveys country statistics like demographics, languages, GDP and passport rankings. The list of countries was chosen based on the top 15 countries listed in the Passport Index 2016 in terms of passport strength.

Legend:
• Order of ring sizes (outer to inner): Passport Index
• Order of arc (from 0 degree clockwise onwards): Nominal GDP per capita (high to low)
• Main languages used in passports shown in shades.
