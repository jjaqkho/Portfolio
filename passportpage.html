A redesign proposal

This is a group project teamed with the amazing Sidney Law and Talia Cotton. Starting as an investigation on typography, this project evolved into a redesign proposal for the Mexican Passport.

To help the current Mexican passport fit into the ever-changing designs of passports globally, multiple research and redesigns have been done in regards to the aesthetic and typography of both the Mexican and other foreign passports.

Although typography was the crux of the project, the focus was mostly on the guilloche illustrations on each spread of the passports. The choice of illustrations are based on features of Mexico.
