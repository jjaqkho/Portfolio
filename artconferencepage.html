Résumé

A reflection of ideas through typography

The design of the poster is for a hypothetical conference on postmodern graphic design. Alongside the poster is a proposed public installation for the event. The objective was to choose one of the speaker's viewpoint and use it as a starting point to your design.
Taking on one of Jeff Keedy's approach on design as a neutral with attention to the invisible grid, I've utilized the grid in attempt to create a box via negative space.

Specifications:
• 18x24
• Double-sided
• Folded into 6x9

Artist biographies and schedule at the back of the poster

Title in the front broken down into syllables and positioned that it can be folded down in 6x9 where syllables are contained in the middle.
*Cyan = fold creases
