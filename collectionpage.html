Résumé

A deconstruction of flags

This project's objective was to hold a collection items and display them in an interactive manner. I've decided to highlight the complexity in flags that are often neglected.

To achieve this, 50 random flags were generated online and displayed in a gridded format. To add more depth to the site, flags were deconstructed according to categories and can be toggled on and off to create a layering/filtering effect.
