A rebranding proposal

The assignment was to chose a restaurant of our choice and rebrand it. As gentrification takes over Williamsburg, I felt that Oasis was the appropriate choice for a rebranding to better fit in with its new gentrified surroundings.

Given my sufficient knowledge in the Arabic language, I've decided to put myself up for the challenge in giving it a Oasis a logo combining both its English and Arabic name.
